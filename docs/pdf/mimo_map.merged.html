Introduction

Filip Koselak

About Mimo

Mimo is one of the most popular coding applications available. It has been downloaded over 10 million times. Its aim is to faciliate coding-learning and to make it accesible for everyone. Mimo offers lessons of web development as wel as Phyton or SQL.

Every subject is devided into small lessons, so that you learn step by step. Apart from the lessons, you can practice what you have learnt with featured projects and coding challenges.

There is also a community feature that enables you to communicate with other users of the application.

Is Mimo for you?

Mimo is a good solution for you if you want to learn coding but you prefer to learn step by step. The material is devided into small lesson allowing you to learn one skill at the time. It is also suitable for you if you do not have a lot of time. With Mimo you can learn anywhere with your mobile phone. It can take only a couple of minutes to finish a single lesson.

How can you benefit from using Mimo?

Mimo helps you to start to learn coding, as well as practicing what you are already familiar with. The content and the structure of the lessons make it easy and fun to learn. If you do them reguralily, you will get the hang of coding in short time.

How do I start using Mimo

Filip Koselak

Mimo setup

These are the steps you should take in order to install Mimo and to create an account.
  1. Open Google Play Store and look for Mimo. Once you find it, click on the "install" button.
  2. Click on the "Get started" button in order to create a new account.
  3. Click on the "Let's go" button to continue creating your account.
  4. Choose one of the following options to state why you are interested in learning to code:
    • To become a professional developer
    • Just for fun
    • For my current job
    • For a different reason.
  5. Choose the option that decribes your occupation from the following options:
    • High school student
    • University student
    • Employee
    • None of those.
  6. Swipe to one of the following options to determine the level of your experience:
    • None
    • A little
    • A lot.
  7. Select one of the following options to state what you want to learn:
    • Web development
    • Python
    • SQL.
  8. Choose one of the following options in order to set the goal:
    • Casual (5 minutes of learning daily)
    • Regular(10 minutes of learning daily)
    • Serious(20 minutes of learning daily).
  9. Set a time for a daily reminder.

How to do a lesson

How to study with Mimo

Filip Koselak

Lesson

This is how to do a lesson.

  1. Click on "Learn" icon below to start a lesson.
  2. Click on the icon with a name of path in the upper left corner.
  3. Chose the path you want to learn from:
    • Web Development
    • Python
    • SQL.
  4. Choose a subject you want to learn by clicking on it. You have to finish all the lessons from a subject to unlock the next one.
  5. Choose a lesson you are interested in. You have to finish a lesson to unlock the next one.
  6. Follow the instructions to complete the task. There are three types of exercises:
    1. Choose the right elements in the right order by clicking on them.
    2. Type the answer that you are asked to give.
    3. In this type of exercises you have to choose a correct answer from the given options.
  7. Use the following buttons to navigate the task:
    1. Click on this button to completely cancel your answer.
    2. Click on this button to cancel only the last element of your answer.
    3. Click on this button to submit your answer.
  8. If the answer is correct, "CONTINUE" button appears. Click on it to continue learning.
  9. If the answer is wrong, "SKIP" and "TRY AGAIN" buttons appear. Click on "SKIP" button if you want to go to another question or click on "TRY AGAIN" button if you want to give a new answer.
  10. Coding/browser view.
  11. After completing the lesson you receive points and coins.
  12. Click on the "Glossary" button (the one with a book) to open glossary.
  13. Click on any element to see its definition.

Other elements of Mimo app

  • Leaderboard

    On leaderboard you can see your current standings, check your league and see your competition. The leaderboard is updated every week. There are five leagues, starting from Wooden. You have to be in the top 10 to advance to the next league.

  • Community

    You can use this feature to communicate with the community of learners and look for useful information.

  • Mimo store

    You receive coins after completing lessons. You can access Mimo Store to exchange your coins for: streak freeze, streak repair and league protection. With streak freeze you can keep your streak going if you have 24 hours of inactivity. You can use streak repair to repair the most recent day of inactivity. With league protection you can protect yourself from dropping to a lower league when you end up in the danger zone.

  • Activity

    Activity tabs shows you the days when you completed lessons. It shows you your current streak as well. The streak is the number of days in a row you have completed a lesson. Your streak will increase by 1 day once you finish a lesson.

  • Playgrounds

    Playground is an option that allows you to practice what you have learned.

Changing the settings

Those are the steps to follow to set your account.

  1. Open settings.
    1. Click on "profile" icon below.
    2. Click on "settings" icon in the upper right corner.
    3. Now you can change the settings.
  2. Change profile photo.
    1. Click on "change profile picture".
    2. Select the source of your new photo.
    3. Select the photo.
    4. Adjust the photo.
    5. Now press on "crop" in the upper right corner.
  3. Set up the sound effects.
    1. Click on the toggle bar to switch it on or off.
  4. Change password.
    1. Click on "change password".
    2. Introduce your e-mail addres in "your email" window.
    3. Click on "reset password" button.
  5. Delete account.
    1. Click on "delete account".
    2. Click on "delete" button to complete the delete.
  6. Log out.
    1. Click on "log out".
